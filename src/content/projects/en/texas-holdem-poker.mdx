A browser-based multiplayer Texas Hold'em platform — no registration, type a
nickname and sit down. Up to nine seats per table, any of them human or AI,
with reconnection handling so a dropped connection doesn't fold your hand.

The part I care most about is the **probability layer**: a Monte-Carlo
simulation engine computes your live win equity as the board develops, a card
tracker keeps the deck state visible, and the decision hints are derived from
the numbers — not vibes. The AI opponents come in three difficulty levels and
play the same probabilistic game you do.

Details that make it feel finished: adaptive background music that follows the
state of the hand, spectator mode for busted players, responsive layout down to
phones.
