A multiplayer platform for three classic board games, playable in the browser
over any local network — where the point is **playing against AI**: every seat
at every board can be taken by a human or an AI opponent, in any combination.

Challenge the AI at gomoku, chess, or xiangqi; mix human-vs-AI and AI-vs-AI
seats at the same table. The AI seats are backed by a local search engine
component with a fixed thinking budget of a few seconds per move — strong
enough to be worth beating.

Built as a home-network project: no accounts, no cloud — open a browser on the
LAN and play.
